About Staffies

Popularity

The Staffordshire Terrier has gained popularity in Ireland over the years. They are often chosen as companion and family dogs. The popularity of the breed has also brought attention to responsible ownership and breed-specific legislation. Educating owners on responsible dog ownership, promoting positive training methods, and advocating for fair legislation are crucial aspects of ensuring the well-being and positive perception of the breed. It's important for potential owners to research and understand the breed's specific needs and requirements.

Temperament

Staffies are friendly and affectionate dogs. They are often referred to as "nanny dogs" because of their love for children and protective instincts. They are generally good-natured, courageous, and eager to please. They are intelligent and can be quick learners. They respond well to positive reinforcement training methods. Early socialization and obedience training are crucial to help them become well-mannered adults.

Energy Level and Exercise

Staffies have a moderate to high energy level. They require regular exercise and mental stimulation to stay happy and healthy. Daily walks, play sessions, and engaging activities are important to meet their exercise needs. They are known for their love of people, including children. They can make excellent family pets when raised in a loving and responsible environment. Proper socialization and training are essential to ensure they are well-behaved around other animals.

Sandy

When Sandy first came to us, she was a malnourished and fearful little girl. She was found on the side of the road with a deep gash along her back left leg. Thanks to some wonderful people, she was promptly taken to a vet for immediate care. Sandy is now spayed, vaccinated, and microchipped. Since then, she has made remarkable progress, and we couldn't be happier with her transformation. Sandy walks excellently on a leash and enjoys playing with a tennis ball. Her crate training is still a work in progress. This sweet girl is now eagerly seeking her forever home where she can continue to flourish and be loved.

    Her ideal home would be;
  • As an only dog
  • Dog savvy kids
  • Cat friendly
  • A small garden to play in and sunbathe
